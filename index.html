Your Health, Our Concern

The Kitui Hospital is a leading private health facility located in Kitui Town, conveniently situated opposite the Kitui County Referral Hospital (CHMT Gate), next to Himalaya Springs Hotel. We are committed to providing comprehensive, compassionate and affordable healthcare to the people of Kitui County and beyond.

Our hospital operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — offering Outpatient, Inpatient, Maternity, Emergency and Specialist services all under one roof. We are equipped with a modern laboratory, theatre, dental suite, OPG, ultrasound, optical services and a well-stocked pharmacy.

We work with a dedicated team of Medical Officers, Clinical Officers, Specialist Surgeons, Nurses, and support staff who share one mission — to deliver quality care with dignity and compassion to every patient who walks through our doors.

The Best Hospital in Kitui — Open 24 Hours, 7 Days a Week

The Kitui Hospital, Maternity and Nursing Home is the leading private hospital and health facility in Kitui Town, Kitui County, Kenya. We are conveniently located opposite Kitui County Referral Hospital (CHMT Gate), next to Himalaya Springs Hotel. Our hospital is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — providing quality, affordable and compassionate healthcare to patients across Kitui County and the wider Eastern Kenya region.

Whether you are looking for a hospital in Kitui, a clinic in Kitui, a maternity hospital in Kitui, or a 24-hour emergency health facility in Kitui County, The Kitui Hospital is your trusted partner for all your healthcare needs. Our services include General Outpatient (OPD), Maternity and Antenatal Care, Inpatient Nursing, Emergency Care, Laboratory, Pharmacy, Theatre and Surgery, Dental Services, Optical Services, Ultrasound, Physiotherapy, ENT, Obstetrics and Gynaecology, Paediatrics, Urology, Orthopaedic Surgery, Maxillofacial Surgery, and Nutrition and Dietetics.

Our dedicated team includes experienced Medical Officers, General Surgeons, Clinical Officers, Registered Nurses, Midwives, Pharmacists, and Laboratory Technicians committed to delivering world-class healthcare right here in Kitui. We accept Britam, CIC Group, Madison, First Assurance, SHA (Social Health Authority / NHIF), Minet and AAR Insurance.
